    Who we are

    Get to know about our company

    Nzuzo Management Services is a professional services company that is 100% owned by black professional over the period in excess of 10 years. The company has been established with the vision of increasing the financial management capability of our clients and also support the small to medium entities in their endeavour to create value for the stakeholders. We work with our clients to develop and deliver sustainable solutions that can assist in the transformation of their financial systems and processes. We always strive to provide simplified solutions for the benefit of our clients through a number of interventions on the areas of risk management, development of processes, enhancement of internal controls and other finance management services. We do acknowledge the increasing pressure on the organisations to create a balance of the strategic and operational focus with the heightened regulatory scrutiny. Our services are based on the principles governed by the International Accounting Standards, King II report on Corporate Governance, Income Tax Act, Companies Act of 2008, Public Finance Management Act and the Municipal Finance Management Act.
